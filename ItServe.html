ITServe, meet Peerlift.

Peerlift is a tech nonprofit that connects underresourced students to opportunities that make college possible. ITServe Alliance is the largest association of IT Services, Staffing, Consulting organizations. As a part of the Corporate Social Responsibility Program, ITServe has partnered with Peerlift to provide financial assistance in developing technologies for easy access of scholarships and outreach programs for low income, first generation students. Please donate at the link below.

Students are struggling

Fifteen million high school students face an average student to counselor ration of 491:1. Many cannot find opportunities like scholarships and internships for college. Even more aren't confident enough to apply.

Where is the money going?

Outreach Events

We want to help low-income, first generation students DIRECTLY through in-person outreach events in the low-income areas of the IT Serve chapter locations: Philadelphia, New Jersey, Detroit, Virginia, Chicago, Atlanta, Dallas, Houston, Tampa, Phoenix, Seattle, and the Bay Area.

At these events, we plan to give presentations about the college application process and give advice about specific programs and career paths.

Product Development

1. We want to showcase local opportunities through web scraping and filtering algorithms.

2. We want to create Peerlift portals for schools in which students can use their accounts and connect with peers and mentors
