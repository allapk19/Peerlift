I'm a College Freshman in Philadelphia
Always curious to learn more about the people and world around me.


Name: Makeda Dereje

Age: 18

Hometown: Atlanta, Georgia

School: University of Pennsylvania

Fun Fact: I am basically obsessed with watching Vox YouTube videos.

Email: makeda@peerlift.org
I went to a public high school where many students, including myself, came from a lower-income family and guidance counselors had limited access to resources. Therefore, many out of school programs weren't an option because of either the cost or lack of knowledge about it. When I wanted to do a summer program after my junior year, I spent days searching online, finally coming across a few that offered generous scholarships and interested me. Additionally, the guidance counselors at my school didn't have much time to help in my college application process and most of their resources were limited to applying to in-state schools, which made finding scholarships that fit my needs even harder.
I discovered PeerLift in the second half of my senior year, wishing it was sooner. I am a Student Ambassador and Community Manager, meaning I spread the mission of PeerLift to high school students, teachers, and counselors. Since my involvement, I have presented to many classes and clubs at my high school as well as worked with others at PeerLift to try to improve our outreach efforts. I am confident PeerLift would have made my high school career simpler and of it's mission of helping high school students gain access to opportunities.

I'm a Senior in Texas
I'm a boba connoisseur, food enthusiast, and someone who loves learning about new discoveries.


Name: Binh Nguyen

Age: 18

Hometown: Houston, TX

School: Davis High School

Fun Fact: I really, really love tacos

Email: binh@peerlift.org
As a low-income, first generation student, I did not quite fully understand the resources available to me when it comes to educational opportunities. Coming from a high school with over 700 students in my senior class, it was difficult to reach my counselor and learn more about scholarships. Fortunately, I discovered Peerlift through my friends and were immediately awed by the organization's mission of bridging the gap between students and enriching opportunities. Joining Peerlift as a Student Ambassador has helped me clarify the ambiguities of applying to college as well as scholarships. Most importantly, I loved being able to advance the mission of equal access to opportunities, especially to students with limited resources.

I'm a Senior in Pittsburgh
I'm a guy that loves to laugh and loves to make other people laugh.


Name: Victor Yu

Age: 17

Hometown: Pittsburgh, PA

School: North Allegheny Senior High School

Fun Fact: I've never played Fortnite

Email: victor@peerlift.org
I was originally born in an low-income neighborhood, where there was little to no support when it came to providing students with resources they would need to succeed in their high school and college years. I am now fortunate enough to be living in a middle-class area, where I have realized how lucky I am to be in this position of helping fellow students. At the same time, it is often frustrating knowing that there are bright, young teenagers that are not able to reach their full potential simply because of where they grew up.
As the Associate Director of Outreach, it is my job to make sure that students are able to have access to all the opportunities they need to be successful. I've made so many new friends and learned so many important values during my time representing Peerlift and encourage anyone who has a passion for education to sign up for the outreach program. I am in charge of the interviews so you'll be talking to me if you do sign up
