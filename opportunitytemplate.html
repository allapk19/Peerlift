US Senate Youth Program

9, 10, 11, 12

Highly Selective

7-Nov

A week long experience in government and leadership

USSYP identifies high school juniors and seniors interested in a career in public service. Students participate in an immersive educational week in DC with government leaders and their peers.

Program Cost:

Aid Breakdown:

For the 2017 summer, more than 50 percent of participants received some form of aid.

Need-based aid is awarded on a first come first serve basis.

How do I get nominated?

Students must be nominated by their principal. Each high school may only nominate one student

When is the program?

Washington Week is completely free of travel and attendance costs, and usually takes place within the first two weeks of March
